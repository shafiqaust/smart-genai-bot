MODULE TITLE: Module #5 - Generative Adversarial Networks

--- SECTION: Overview ---

We will discuss Generative Adversarial Networks (GAN) in this module. We will build different types of GANs from the scratch using Keras, discuss their advantages, and disadvantages.

--- SECTION: Learning Objectives ---

By the end of this module, you should be able to:

MLO 1: Learn about the architectural design of a generative adversarial network (GAN).

MLO 2: Build and train various types of GAN (deep convolutional GAN (DCGAN), Wasserstein GAN (WGAN), conditional GAN (CGAN)) from scratch using Keras, and use them to generate new images.

MLO 3: Understand the strengths and weaknesses of each type of GAN.
