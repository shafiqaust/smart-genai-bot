Module: Agentic AI

--- SECTION: Overview ---

In this module, we will discuss Agentic AI Agents. Focus will be on understanding the motivation of this type of agents and discussing about architectures, characteristics, and applications of agentic AI.

--- SECTION: Learning Objectives ---

Content

By the end of this module, you should be able to:

MLO 1: Understand the notion of an autonomous agent, how to evaluate an agent
MLO 2: Understand the architectures of agents
MLO 3: Understand the difference between agentic AI Agents and classical autonomous agents
MLO 4: Be able to develop an agentic AI agent
