MODULE TITLE: Advanced GAN

--- SECTION: Overview ---

In this module, we will learn about different GAN models that are behind state-of-the-art image generation software. We will discuss ProGAN, StyleGAN, StylGAN2, BigGAN, and how Transformer is used in training ViT VQ-GAN.

--- SECTION: Learning Objectives ---

By the end of this module, you should be able to:

MLO 1: See how a ProGAN model progressively trains a GAN to generate high resolution images.

MLO 2: Understand how ProGAN was adapted to build StyleGAN, a high-performing GAN for image synthesis, and how this was adjusted to create StyleGAN2, a state-of-the-art model that improves further upon the original work.

MLO 3: Understand the architecture of the Self-Attention GAN (SAGAN), which incorporates the attention mechanism into the GAN framework and how BigGAN expands upon the ideas in the SAGAN paper to produce high quality images.

MLO 4: Learn how VQ-GAN uses a codebook to encode images into a discrete sequence of tokens that can be modeled using a Transformer and how was it adapted to create ViT VQ-GAN using Transformers instead of convolutional layers in the encoder and decoder.
