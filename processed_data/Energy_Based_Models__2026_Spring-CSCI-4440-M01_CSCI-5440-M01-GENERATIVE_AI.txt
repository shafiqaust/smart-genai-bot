MODULE TITLE: Energy Based Models

--- SECTION: Overview ---
In this module, we will learn about Energy Based Models (EBM), a different type of models for generative AI. We will discuss the motivation behind this type of models, its architecture, and an implementation of an EBM working with the MNIST dataset.

--- SECTION: Learning Objectives ---
By the end of this module, you should be able to:

MLO 1: Understand how to formulate a deep energy-based model (EBM).
MLO 2: See how to sample from an EBM using Langevin dynamics.
MLO 3: Train your own EBM using contrastive divergence.
MLO 4: Analyze the EBM, including viewing snapshots of the Langevin dynamics process.
MLO 5: Learn about other type of EBM such as restricted Boltzmann machines.
