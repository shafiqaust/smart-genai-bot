MODULE TITLE: Normalizing Floow Models

--- SECTION: Overview ---

In this module, we will learn about Normalizing Floow Models, especially the RealNVP model. We will discuss the motivation behind this type of models, its architecture, and an implementation of RealNVP.

--- SECTION: Learning Objectives ---

CATEGORY: Content

By the end of this module, you should be able to:

MLO 1: Understand the motivation for normalizing flow models and the difference between the three types of models (VAE, GAN, and normalizing flow)

MLO 2: Learn about the Jacobian determinant

MLO 3: Build a RealNVP model using Keras and Tensorflow
