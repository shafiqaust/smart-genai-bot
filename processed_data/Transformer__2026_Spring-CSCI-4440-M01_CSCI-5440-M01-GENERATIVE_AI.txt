MODULE: Transformer

SECTION: Overview
In this module, we will learn about Transformer, the architecture behinds all the famous large language models such as GPT, BERT, T5, etc. We will discuss the key ideas of the architecture and an implementation of a transformer that generates images of flowers.

SECTION: Learning Objectives
By the end of this module, you should be able to:

MLO 1: Learn conceptually how an attention mechanism mimics our way of attaching more importance to some words in a sentence than others.

MLO 2: Understand the attention mechanism works from first principles, including how queries, keys, and values are created and manipulated.

MLO 3: Learn the importance of causal masking for text generation tasks.

MLO 4: Understand how attention heads can be grouped into a multihead attention layer and how they form one part of a Transformer block.

MLO 5: Build a GPT model in Keras to generate the text contained in wine reviews.
